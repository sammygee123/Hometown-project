Top three activities to do in Bath

Holburn Museum

The Holburn Museum is a grade 1 listed building that used to be the home of Lady Grey and is now home to some of the most fasinating art exhibitions in the country. This building was also recently seen on the Netflix series Bridgerton!

The Roman Baths

The Roman Baths, almost over 2000 years old and is one of the most important historial places in the whole of england. This site among a few temples is an insight to the origins of the city of Bath and the people that lived here.
